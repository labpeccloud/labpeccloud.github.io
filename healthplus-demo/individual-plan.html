Plan Overview

Our Individual Plan provides comprehensive coverage for your healthcare needs, with flexible options to fit your lifestyle and budget. Designed for individuals who want quality healthcare without unnecessary extras.

Primary Care

Unlimited access to in-network primary care physicians with low copays. Build a relationship with a doctor who knows your health history and can coordinate your care.

Prescription Coverage

Comprehensive prescription drug coverage with affordable copays for generic medications and reasonable costs for brand-name drugs.

Hospital Services

Coverage for inpatient and outpatient hospital services, emergency room visits, and surgical procedures with manageable deductibles.

Preventive Care

100% coverage for preventive services including annual check-ups, immunizations, screenings, and wellness visits to help you stay healthy.

Mental Health

Access to mental health professionals including therapists, psychologists, and psychiatrists with the same copays as primary care visits.

Digital Access

24/7 virtual care through our mobile app, online portal for managing appointments, prescriptions, and claims, plus digital health tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for the Individual Health Plan?

Any individual who is not eligible for Medicare and does not have access to employer-sponsored insurance can apply for our Individual Health Plan. This includes self-employed individuals, freelancers, part-time workers, and those between jobs.

Can I keep my current doctor?

You can continue seeing your current doctor if they are part of our network. Our Individual Plan has one of the largest provider networks in the country, so there's a good chance your doctor is already in-network. You can use our Provider Search tool to check if your doctor is in our network before enrolling.

Are pre-existing conditions covered?

Yes, all pre-existing conditions are covered under our Individual Health Plan. We do not exclude or limit coverage for any health condition you had before your coverage started.

How do I enroll in the Individual Health Plan?

Enrolling is simple! You can enroll online through our website, call our enrollment specialists at 1-800-555-HEALTH, or schedule an appointment with one of our local insurance consultants. The enrollment process typically takes 15-20 minutes to complete.

When can I enroll in an Individual Health Plan?

You can enroll during the annual Open Enrollment Period (typically November through January) or if you qualify for a Special Enrollment Period due to a qualifying life event such as losing other coverage, moving, getting married, having a baby, or adopting a child.

Can I cancel my plan if I get employer coverage?

Yes, you can cancel your Individual Health Plan at any time. If you obtain employer-sponsored health insurance or become eligible for other coverage, you can terminate your Individual Plan without penalty. Just be sure to time the cancellation to avoid any gaps in coverage.
