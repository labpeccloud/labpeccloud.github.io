About HealthPlus

Committed to providing affordable healthcare solutions since 2010

Our Story

HealthPlus was founded in 2010 with a clear mission: to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone. What started as a small team of healthcare professionals has grown into one of the most trusted names in health insurance.

Over the years, we've expanded our coverage options while maintaining our commitment to personalized care and customer satisfaction. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthcare that meets their unique needs without breaking the bank.

Today, HealthPlus serves over 1 million members across the country, offering a wide range of insurance plans for individuals, families, seniors, and employers.

Our Mission & Values

Care Focused

We put our members' health and well-being at the center of everything we do, ensuring that care is accessible when it's needed most.

Trust & Integrity

We believe in transparency and honesty in all our dealings, building lasting relationships with our members and healthcare partners.

Affordability

We are committed to making quality healthcare affordable for all, with plans designed to fit every budget without sacrificing quality.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to improve our services and embrace technology to enhance the member experience.
