Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for offering group health insurance?

To qualify for group health insurance, your business typically needs to have at least two employees (including the owner in some cases). You'll need to contribute a minimum percentage toward employee premiums (usually at least 50%), and a minimum percentage of eligible employees must participate in the plan (typically 70%).

Documentation requirements include your business tax ID, payroll records, and employee census data. Our team can guide you through the specific requirements based on your business size and location.

How much does group health insurance cost?

The cost of group health insurance varies based on several factors, including:

  • Your location
  • The size of your group
  • The age and health profile of your employees
  • The level of coverage you choose
  • Your contribution strategy (how much you pay vs. how much employees pay)

On average, employers can expect to pay anywhere from $300 to $600 per employee per month for comprehensive group coverage. We offer free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your specific business situation.

Can we offer different plans to different employees?

Yes, you can offer different plans to different employee classes, provided these classifications are based on legitimate business categories such as:

  • Full-time vs. part-time status
  • Geographic location
  • Job categories or departments
  • Length of service (for new hires vs. established employees)

However, you cannot discriminate based on health status or other protected characteristics. Our benefits consultants can help you design a multi-plan strategy that complies with all applicable regulations while meeting the diverse needs of your workforce.

What is the enrollment process like?

The enrollment process for HealthPlus Employer Plans is straightforward:

  1. Consultation: We'll work with you to understand your needs and budget.
  2. Plan Selection: We'll help you choose the right plan(s) for your business.
  3. Employee Communication: We provide materials to explain benefits to your team.
  4. Online Enrollment: Employees can enroll through our user-friendly portal.
  5. Ongoing Support: We help manage changes throughout the year.

The initial enrollment typically takes 2-4 weeks from application to coverage effective date. We also provide annual open enrollment support to help employees review and update their selections.

What tax benefits are available for offering health insurance?

Businesses can realize several tax advantages by offering group health insurance:

  • Tax-Deductible Premiums: Employer contributions to health insurance are typically 100% tax-deductible as a business expense.
  • FICA Tax Savings: Premiums paid by employers are exempt from federal payroll taxes, saving approximately 7.65% on those dollars.
  • Small Business Health Care Tax Credit: Eligible small businesses may qualify for a tax credit of up to 50% of premium costs.
  • Pre-Tax Employee Contributions: When employees pay their portion of premiums with pre-tax dollars, it reduces their taxable income and saves both the employee and employer on FICA taxes.

We recommend consulting with your tax advisor to understand the specific tax implications for your business.

Can we customize our plan with additional benefits?

Absolutely! HealthPlus offers a wide range of supplemental benefits that can be added to your group health plan:

  • Dental and Vision Plans: Comprehensive coverage for oral and eye health.
  • Life and Disability Insurance: Financial protection for employees and their families.
  • Employee Assistance Programs (EAP): Mental health and counseling services.
  • Health Savings Accounts (HSAs) and Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs): Tax-advantaged accounts for healthcare expenses.
  • Wellness Programs: Customized initiatives to improve employee health and reduce costs.

These additional benefits can be offered on an employer-paid, shared-cost, or voluntary (employee-paid) basis, giving you flexibility in designing a comprehensive benefits package that fits your budget.
