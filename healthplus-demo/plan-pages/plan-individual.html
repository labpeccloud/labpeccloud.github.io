Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if this plan is right for me?

The Essential Care plan is ideal for individuals who need basic coverage at an affordable price. It's best suited for those who are generally healthy, primarily need preventive care, and want protection against unexpected medical expenses. If you require regular specialist visits or have ongoing prescription needs, you might consider our Enhanced or Premier Care plans instead.

Can I keep my current doctor?

You can keep your current doctor if they are in our Standard Provider Network. Before enrolling, we recommend using our Provider Search tool to check if your preferred doctors and hospitals are in-network. If you see an out-of-network provider, you'll likely pay more for services.

When will my coverage begin?

If you enroll during the Open Enrollment Period, your coverage will typically begin on January 1st. If you enroll during a Special Enrollment Period due to a qualifying life event, your coverage will usually begin on the first day of the month following your enrollment.

How do I refill prescriptions with this plan?

Prescription refills can be processed through any in-network pharmacy. You can also use our online prescription refill service or mail-order pharmacy for convenience and potential cost savings. Simply visit our Prescription Refill page to get started.

Can I add optional dental or vision coverage?

Yes, we offer optional dental and vision coverage that can be added to your Essential Care plan for an additional monthly premium. These supplemental plans provide coverage for routine eye exams, glasses, contacts, dental cleanings, fillings, and more.

Plan Eligibility

Who is eligible for the Essential Care plan?

This plan is ideal for individuals who:

  • Are between the ages of 18-64
  • Don't have access to employer-sponsored insurance
  • Aren't eligible for Medicare or Medicaid
  • Need affordable, basic coverage for essential health benefits
  • Are generally healthy and primarily need preventive care

Open enrollment typically runs from November to January, but you may qualify for a Special Enrollment Period if you've experienced certain life events like losing other coverage, moving, getting married, having a baby, or adopting a child.
